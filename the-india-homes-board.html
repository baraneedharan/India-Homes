IndiaHomes is a professionally managed company and its key members are drawn from diverse industries directly and indirectly related to the sector. The company maintains the highest level of management and financial integrity and the entire IndiaHomes Management Team is committed to building a professional company keeping with international best practices in the property services business. The Professional Management Team of IndiaHomes is guided by the Board of Directors and Board of Advisors of IndiaHomes who are renowned business professionals of India and International corporate world.

Ashu Garg

General Partner, Foundation Capital

With a focus on Digital Media and India (across sectors), Ashu brings to IndiaHomes a wide range of experience at global enterprises that spans business operations in the U.S. and India, from product conceptualization to customer acquisition. Besides IndiaHomes, Ashu currently serves on the board of directors of TreeHouse, TubeMogul, Conviva, Aspire Human Capital Management.

Bala Deshpande

Senior Managing Director, NEA

Bala joined New Enterprise Associates India Pvt. Ltd. (NEA India) in 2008 as Senior Managing Director based out of its Mumbai office. Since that time NEA India’s assets under management have grown from ~$100mn to over ~$350mn. Under Bala’s leadership NEA has invested in several companies like Air Works, FSS, InTarvo, Intellisys, Naaptol, Nova, Trishe and Vishwa. Her private equity experience has seen the full cycle from nurturing young companies to executing multiple exits including IPO’s, buy-backs, strategic sales, and capital market divestments. She is also an Independent Director Board Member of Future Retail and Info Edge who are listed on National Stock Exchange and Leaders in their sectors.

Samarjit Singh

Director, IndiaHomes

Educated at the prestigious St. Stephens College, Delhi and the UC Berkeley USA, Samar has 16 yrs of operating experience in building professional organizations that have the ability to scale with speed and professional excellence. Widely regarded as one of India's foremost young business leaders focused on building pioneering organizations that transform industry sectors. Previously the Founder and Managing Director of Candid Marketing, India's number one Marketing Services Company as ranked by the Economic Times Brand Equity Agency Ranking 2010.
Currently the President of APP- Association of Property Professionals and the Director of IndiaHomes, Samar has also served on the Board of the Entrepreneurs Organization (EO) for 7 years and served as President of Board of EO New Delhi in 2007.

Sanjeev Aggarwal

Sr. Managing Director, Helion Advisors
