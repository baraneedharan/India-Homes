IndiaHomes Review

The Heartsong is developed by Experion and is located at Sector 108, Gurgaon.

Location:- The Heartsong, a residential development that offers you an excellent habitation at sector 108 Gurgaon, Dwarka Expressway. This project offers an easy access via 60m and 75m Northern Periphery Road & is spread over 15 acres. The Heartsong is 10 mins from Delhi border, 15 mins from Ansal Plaza and 5 mins from Dwarka Metro Station. It is located in the vicinity of Cyber City and Gurgaon Toll.

Project:- This project offers homes built with quality material and finest of the fixtures and furnishings. Ample spacing and greenery in this project makes it a healthy environment to live in. Convenient Shops and Departmental Stores within the complex, single point access gated community with 24 x 7 manned security & monitored access/exit are more of the offerings here.

Amenities:- The Heartsong by Experion offers excellent and exclusively designed world-class specifications equipped with all sorts of modern amenities such as 24x7 manned securities, grand clubhouse and massive green area. Your lifestyle is adorned with an upmarket specification like the imported marble flooring, wooden flooring in bedrooms, kitchens with granite counters & top of the line washroom fittings, exclusive club house and health club. Moving forward, you can avail umpteen greenery, landscaped gardens with traced jogging tracks, skating rink, cricket nets, pool tables, card area, health club sauna, Jacuzzi, swimming pool, skating rink, lawn tennis court, pool tables and coffee shops, kids play area & and traffic free podium.

Developer:- Experion Group is FDI of Experion Holdings Pvt Ltd, Singapore. It began its operations in India in the year 2006 in real estate and hospitality sectors. Till date it has made investments of more than USD 230 million in acquiring planned project sites at various locations across India and will be developing those as niche assets across numerous segments.
