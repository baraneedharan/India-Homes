Gurgaon, better known as the millennium city, is a burgeoning real estate destination today. As an emerging popular real estate destination, Gurgaon surely has something for all residents and investors alike. Statistically, in the last 5 years, Gurgaon real estate has recorded a tremendous growth in both residential and commercial projects, creating a golden opportunity for an army of leading property developers in Gurgaon. The developers are successfully serving to the rising demands for a variety of residential options such as apartments, villas, penthouses, duplexes and plots.

Gurgaon Real Estate - A Happening Story in Making!
Gurgaon real estate epitomizes a world-class infrastructure by embodying the best in terms of architectural aesthetics, landscaping sensibility, property planning, amenities etc. Not to forget, expanding the reach of Metro connectivity, close proximity to Delhi and customized residential options have even aggravated the charm of Gurgaon for all kinds of real estate investments. Fuelled by the above stated reasons, Gurgaon residential sector has nearly attained an estimated hike of 20-30% in the year 2012-13 especially in areas near to IT/ITES hubs. Also, the rapidly developing areas of Gurgaon are the major investment options as compared to the established ones. It is mainly because these areas are an extension of the city’s infrastructure and even priced lower than the city’s central locations. Thanks to the enabling transport channels, connectivity to far-away locations is an affordable reality.

To cater to the rising needs of growing residents and investors, the city bestows a range of affordable options, developed by some of the leading real-estate brands like DLF, Unitech, Raheja Developers Limited, Tata Housing, BPTP, Omkar realtors, Sunteck real estate, Ekta developers, Ajmera developers, Vatika Group, Puri Construction, Emaar MGF, Ansal API, Sikka Kaamna Green, Salarpuria, Prestige Group, Brigade Group etc. In fact, to survive the fierce competition of real estate, the list of real estate developers in Gurgaon is leaving no stone unturned to transform the idea of a luxury living in your neighborhood. In lieu of promoting the concept of luxury living, developers are increasingly adding smarter housing options in their offerings. As a result, newly constructed apartments and housing options in Gurgaon reflects a judicious blend of world-class infrastructure, living standards, luxury, architecture, amenities, services and pricing. This unique approach has embarked a sense of personal touch and appeal into every housing option, enabling residents to realize their choice & voice while choosing a perfect home.
